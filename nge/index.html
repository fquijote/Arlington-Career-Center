Neon Genesis Evangelion is a Japanese anime series created by Gainax that began in October 1995. The anime was written and directed by Hideaki Anno, and co-produced by TV Tokyo and Nihon Ad Systems. It gained international renown and won several animation awards, and was the start of the Neon Genesis Evangelion series.
